# Non-Expert User Test Guide

Use this for five short feedback sessions with curious users who are not crypto or Stellar experts.

## Goal

Learn whether a user understands the value promise in under 20 seconds: describe a need, let the agent find a digital service, review a demo payment proposal, and decide whether to approve or discard it.

## Two-Minute Flow

1. Ask the tester to open `https://agente-pagos-stellar.vercel.app`.
2. After 20 seconds, ask what they think the product does before explaining anything.
3. Ask them to use the main request box or choose one visible option:
   - `Analizar una transaccion`
   - `Extraer datos de una web`
   - `Comprar creditos de API`
   - `Preparar un sandbox MCP`
   - `Generar un audio corto`
   - `Crear una imagen para demo`
4. Ask them to open `Revisar propuesta` and explain what they think will happen if they approve.
5. Ask them to approve the demo payment or discard it.
6. Ask them to open `Activity`, read the contextual feedback prompt, and leave anonymous feedback from Home.

## Interview Questions

- Que crees que hace esta app?
- Que servicio le pedirias primero al agente?
- Entendiste que pasaba al aprobar el pago de prueba?
- Que parte te dio desconfianza o te hizo dudar?
- La usarias para pagos reales pequenos si estuviera pulida y revisada?

## Measure

- Did they understand the product without an explanation?
- Did `Modo demo` and `Pago de prueba` feel clear?
- Did they understand that the browser cannot move funds by itself?
- Which words caused hesitation?
- Would they trust this flow for a real low-value payment after more polish?

## Safety Rules

Do not request private keys, emails, RUT, phone numbers, account IDs, customer identifiers or card data. Do not open submit gates. Do not present simulated receipts as real settlement.
