# Pilot Test Guide

Use this guide for the first 5-10 feedback sessions. The goal is learning, not moving funds.

## Public link

https://agente-pagos-stellar.vercel.app

## What to ask testers to do

1. Open the Home page and say what the product does in their own words after 20 seconds.
2. Ask the agent for a digital service, for example: `Analizar una transaccion`, `Extraer informacion de una web` or `Comprar creditos de API`.
3. Open `Review` and inspect what the agent wants to buy, the amount, policy checks, privacy proof and human approval boundary.
4. Open `Activity` or `Evidence` and verify what is public vs private.
5. Submit the anonymous feedback form on Home.

## What we measure

- Clarity: did they understand the product without a live explanation?
- Trust: would they approve an agent-prepared payment?
- Friction: which label, route or status confused them?
- Buyer signal: would they use it for MCP/API spend, provider payments or internal agent tooling?
- Partner signal: would a provider want to expose a paid resource through this flow?

## Safety boundaries

- Do not ask testers for private keys, emails, RUT, account numbers, phone numbers or customer identifiers.
- Do not enable submit gates during feedback sessions.
- Treat this as a supervised Stellar testnet demo.
- Keep multichain as hidden lab context, not the public pitch.
